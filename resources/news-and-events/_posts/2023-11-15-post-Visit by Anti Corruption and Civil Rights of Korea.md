<p>On 15 November 2023, a delegation from the Anti-Corruption and Civil Rights
Commission (ACRC) of the Republic of Korea visited AGO. The delegates are:
ACRC’s Deputy Director Mr Jeong Cheol-woo, and Assistant Director Ms Lee
Ga-yeon.</p>
<p>Acting Group Director Christopher Tang shared with the delegates on AGO’s
role and work. Both parties also shared on their respective agencies’ initiatives
to drive digitalisation efforts and enhance work efficiency.</p>
