<p>On 22 November 2023, Mr Hazem Saad Al Qethami, Executive Deputy for Planning
and Development from the General Court of Audit of the Kingdom of Saudi
Arabia (SAI Saudi Arabia) visited AGO.</p>
<p>After a warm welcome from Assitant Auditor-General Mabel Watt, AGO officials
shared on AGO’s role and work, AGO’s digitalisation journey, AGO Academy’s
training roadmap for auditors and the AGO Strategic Workforce Plan. During
the meeting, the two SAIs shared on various aspects of their respective
transformation efforts.</p>
