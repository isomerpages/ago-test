<p>On 22 Nov 2023, Mr Hazem Saad Al Qethami, Executive Deputy for Planning
and Development from the General Court of Audit of the Kingdom of Saudi
Arabia (SAI Saudi Arabia) visited AGO.</p>
<p>After a warm welcome from AAG Mabel Watt, Dir Cherrie Ng Hui Jing (AGO)
gave a presentation on AGO’s role and work. SAD Khoo Wen Yong (AGO) then
shared on AGO’s digitalisation journey while DD Sabrina Fok (AGO) presented
AGO Academy’s training roadmap for auditors and the AGO Strategic Workforce
Plan. During the meeting, the two SAIs shared on various aspects of their
respective transformation efforts.</p>
