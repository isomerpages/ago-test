<h4><strong>Apart from fresh graduates, AGO is also looking for experienced professionals to join our dynamic team of senior auditors.</strong></h4>
</blockquote>
<p>Our senior auditors form the leadership group that strategises, plans
and leads teams in public sector auditing. Our job as national auditors
is noble, satisfying and meaningful. We take particular pride in our strong
people development system that balances professional development with personal
well-being. If you have at least 15 years of audit or related experience
and are game for a career switch, please contact us at <a href="mailto:AGO_HROD@ago.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">AGO_HROD@ago.gov.sg</a>.</p>
