<blockquote><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Office shoot/lowres2Z0A7189_800x400.jpg"></div><h4><strong>AGO Academy is the professional training arm of the Auditor-General's Office (AGO).</strong></h4></blockquote><p>The setting up of the Academy underscores AGO's commitment towards the professional development and upskilling of our staff. In addition, the Academy also aims to improve public sector governance in Singapore by educating our public sector officers on governance and accountability issues.</p><blockquote><h4><strong>Our Mission and Vision</strong></h4></blockquote><p>To be a centre of excellence for training in public sector auditing.</p><p>The Academy's responsibilities include:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Identifying the training and development needs of AGO officers;</p></li><li><p>Developing a training roadmap for AGO officers; and</p></li><li><p>Designing and implementing training curriculum for AGO officers.</p></li></ul><p></p>
